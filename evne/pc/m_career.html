We're hiring!

VnExpress International is looking for young and creative story tellers with excellent English to join our teams in Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City.

Successful applicants will be working full time in an open, fast-paced and highly demanding environment. Check out our site to have an idea of what sort of work you could be doing: e.vnexpress.net.

Salary: competitive

Requirements:

Reporters for News, Business and Travel&Life:
  • University degree or higher in any field
  • Excellent English
  • Excellent storytelling and critical thinking
  • Good knowledge of relevant fields and genuine interest in current affairs
  • Intermediate photography, video shooting and editing skills (Photoshop, Adobe Premiere, After Effects…)
Videographers:
  • University degree or higher in any field
  • Good English
  • Excellent storytelling, critical thinking
  • Creative flair, versatility, conceptual/visual ability and originality
  • Advanced photography, video shooting and editing skills (Adobe Premiere, After Effects…)
  • Broad general knowledge and genuine interest in current affairs
Animators:
  • University degree or higher in any field
  • Good English
  • Excellent storytelling, critical thinking
  • Creative flair, versatility, conceptual/visual ability and originality
  • Advanced animation video editing skills
  • Up to date with industry leading software and technologies (In Design, Illustrator, Dreamweaver, Photoshop…)
  • - Broad general knowledge and genuine interest and curiosity in current affairs

How to apply:

Email your CV and samples of relevant work

by March 31, 2017 to international@vnexpress.net
